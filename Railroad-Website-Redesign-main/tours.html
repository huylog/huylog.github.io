Tours!

THE Pump Car Tour!

"Your chariot awaits you"

"Embark on an exhilarating journey through rail history with our unique Pump Cart Tour at the Springfield Railroad Historical Museum. Feel the rush of the wind as you manually propel our authentic pump carts along the historic tracks, immersing yourself in an interactive and hands-on exploration of the railroad's golden era. Experience the thrill of being in control, surrounded by the scenic beauty of our museum grounds. Our expert guides will regale you with fascinating stories of the St. Louis San-Francisco Railroad and other iconic railroads, adding depth to your adventure. Join us for an unforgettable day filled with the camaraderie of fellow enthusiasts, the rhythm of the tracks, and the joy of reliving the timeless spirit of rail travel. Seize the opportunity to be a part of this unique and unforgettable Pump Cart Tour an experience that promises both excitement and a deep connection to the rich tapestry of railroad history."

The Max Jahn Building Tour

"Type Type Type"

"Join your friends on a journey through the heart of rail history with our exclusive Max Jahn Building Tour at the Springfield Railroad Historical Museum. Discover the hidden gems within the Max Jahn Building, home to a treasure trove of artifacts, equipment, and rare exhibits that showcase the fascinating evolution of rail transport. Join our knowledgeable guides as they unveil the captivating stories behind each exhibit, providing unparalleled insights into the St. Louis San-Francisco Railroad and other iconic railways. Immerse yourself in the authentic ambiance of the Max Jahn Building, a haven for true rail enthusiasts seeking an intimate encounter with the past. Don't miss the chance to delve deep into the legacy of railroading, surrounded by the echoes of locomotives that have shaped history. Choose the Max Jahn Building Tour for a truly immersive and enriching experience, where every corner holds a piece of the extraordinary tale of rail travel."
