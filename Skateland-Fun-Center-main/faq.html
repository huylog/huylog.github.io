FAQs

How much are inlines and speed skates?

$3.00 per pair

How much are skatemates?

$4.00 per set

What forms of payment do you take?

We take cash, checks, and card or apple pay. *Using a card or apple pay does have a small service charge.

How far in advance do I need to book a party?

Most public parties may e booked within 2 weeks of the party date. Pricate parties need to be booked at least 2 months in advance. This extra time ensures your reservation, private parties are limited and book up quickly.

May I walk on the floor with my child?

Yes, but stay near the wall for you and your childs safety.

May I request a song?

Yes, only if your song is family friendly.

May I bring my own skates?

Yes, but we must check them to ensure they will be safe for our skate floor.

May I come in without paying?

If you are younger than 18 without a guardian staying, then you must pay. If you are 18 or older or have a guardian staying, then you do not have to pay.

May I step outside?

We do not allow anyone under the age of 18 outside our door unless they drove here and are leaving. For a smoke break, you must be over the age of 21. No skates are to be worn outside even if they are yours.

May I use this free or BOGO skate pass?

On our normal public session hours, yes. During any special session, no.

Do you do school, home school, church skates?

We can, those wuld have to be booked with Cathy by whoever oversees the event. These are different costs depending on what is or not included.

How long have you been in business?

The building was built in 1988 and our first opening on Friday, January 13th 1989.
